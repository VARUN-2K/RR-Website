Golf Course Extension Road "New Chapter for Luxury Housing"

2020, a year full of shocks, surprises, and amazements as many events happened this year which seemed next to impossible before. The novel coronavirus had a drastic impact on the economical and political front. Despite the economy crashing down, the sales of the housing sector and the real estate business have grown in the past few months. The reason for this boom would be that the people, now, want security in terms of shelter (house). The reason for this sudden need for security has risen because of the current pandemic situation. To fulfill the need for housing, various properties in Gurugram are coming up and out of this pool of properties, golf course extension road serves to be one of the luxurious ones.

Why go for Golf Course Extension Road?

Golf course extension road would be one of the posh areas of Gurugram, even, the location of this housing project is the prime one. The location serves to be the prime one because it is only a 30-minutes drive from the Indira Gandhi International Airport, also, has easy accessibility to NH-8 and South Delhi through the Gurgaon-Faridabad Expressway

The legendary projects like the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC) will boost the extent of the area. Further, the Dwarka-Gurgaon expressway, the yellow and the rapid line metro link, and the western and eastern peripheral expressway have contributed to the prime location of the area.

ALL ABOUT GOLF COURSE EXTENSION

The well-known Sectors 55 and 56 areas along with the Golf Course Extension Road already had a variety of cooperative group housing societies that attracted a vast population in the area. Moreover, the top-notch realty players have invested tons in the area. The list of the investors and housing localities is long including DLF’s Aralias and Belaire, M3M’s Golf Estate, Pioneer Group’s Pioneer Park, TATA’s Raisina Residency, IREO’s Grand Arch, Emaar MGF’s Emerald Hills, and Ansal API’s Esencia. Mostly the above-mentioned housing sectors consist of 3- or 4-BHK luxury apartments of 1,500-2,500 sq ft for the base rate of ₹ 11,000 sq ft.

Sectors from 58-67 were developed along with the commercial projects like Global Business Foyer, Suncity Business Park, Paras Twin Towers, Emerald Plaza Mall, Ninex Time Towers, and many more. The golden features of this area would be a 60-meter-wide road, laid emphasis on security, health, and education, interconnected roads like the Gurgaon-Sona road (running from Hotel Bristol Chowk to M.G. Road), and sufficient parking space for vehicles.
